# MeuQR — Verticals Config Map

This document charts the module allocations, public page sections, and onboarding questionnaires for the 10 business categories.

## 1. Restaurant / Food (`food_beverage`)
* **Core Focus**: Menus, digital ordering, and table/delivery logistics.
* **Default Modules**: `menu`, `orders`, `whatsapp_actions`, `coupons`, `loyalty`, `analytics`.
* **Public Page Sections**: `hero`, `menu_list`, `order_form`, `whatsapp_cta`, `business_hours`.
* **Onboarding Questions**:
  - Offers delivery? (Boolean)
  - Allows pickup? (Boolean)
  - Food Category (Pizzaria, Hamburgueria, Cafeteria, etc.)

## 2. Construction Material Shop (`construction`)
* **Core Focus**: High average tickets, selling by customized quote requests.
* **Default Modules**: `products`, `quote_requests`, `whatsapp_actions`, `customers`, `orders`, `analytics`.
* **Public Page Sections**: `hero`, `product_grid`, `quote_form`, `whatsapp_cta`, `business_hours`.
* **Onboarding Questions**:
  - Quote-based sales? (Boolean)
  - Material deliveries? (Boolean)
  - Department Niche (Material básico, Acabamento, Ferragens, Tintas)

## 3. Clinic / Dentist (`health`)

* **Onboarding Questions**:

  - Telemedicine? (Boolean)
  - Medical Specialty (Dentista, Clínico geral, Fisioterapeuta)
