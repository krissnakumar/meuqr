# 📅 MeuQR Appointments CRM Guide

Welcome to the **Appointments CRM** for MeuQR! This system transforms standard QR pages into a powerful scheduling platform, perfect for clinics, salons, consulting, and any service-based business.

---

## 🌟 Detailed Features

### 1. Unified Dashboard Management
* **Centralized View:** See all upcoming appointments across all your business units from one screen.
* **Smart Statuses:** Track appointments as `Pendente` (Pending), `Confirmado` (Confirmed), `Concluído` (Completed), `Cancelado` (Cancelled), or `Não Compareceu` (No Show).
* **One-Click WhatsApp Integration:** A dedicated button allows you to instantly open a pre-filled WhatsApp message to confirm the appointment directly with the customer.
* **Quick Actions:** Confirm or cancel appointments directly from the table with a single click.

### 2. Public Booking Interface
* **Interactive Drawer Form:** Customers click "Agendar Horário" on your public page, opening a sleek, mobile-friendly drawer to select their date and time.
* **Customer Data Collection:** Captures the customer's name, WhatsApp number, and any special notes or requests.
* **Seamless Handoff:** Once the customer submits the form, it automatically opens WhatsApp on their device to send a confirmation request to your business, while simultaneously saving the booking in your database.

### 3. Dynamic Templates Support
* **Healthcare Templates Included:** Comes pre-configured with templates for "Clínica Médica Geral" and "Clínica de Ortodontia", automatically set up to use the `booking` form type.
* **Extensible:** Any template can use the `booking` form type, instantly enabling the scheduling drawer for that page.

### 4. Robust Database Architecture
* **`appointment_services`:** Stores the services offered, their price, and duration.
* **`staff_members`:** Manages professionals (doctors, stylists, etc.) assigned to services.
* **`business_availability`:** Handles the weekly operating hours for the business or specific staff.
* **`appointments`:** The core ledger storing every booking, securely linked to your business ID with strict Row Level Security (RLS).
  * Public reads are limited to active services from active businesses.
  * Public booking inserts validate `business_id` + `service_id` consistency and only allow `pending` status.

## 🔄 User Workflow: How to Use It

### **Phase 1: Customer Booking (The Front-End Flow)**
1. **Scan & Browse:** A customer scans your QR code and opens your MeuQR public page (e.g., a Dental Clinic template).
2. **Select Service:** The customer taps **"Agendar Horário"**.
3. **Fill the Form:** The sleek booking drawer slides up. The customer enters:
   * Date & Time
   * Name & WhatsApp Number
   * Any observations
4. **Submit & Send:** The customer taps **Confirmar Agendamento**.
   * *Behind the scenes:* The booking is saved securely to Supabase with a `pending` status.
   * *User experience:* WhatsApp opens automatically on the customer's phone with a pre-written message confirming their request, allowing them to send it directly to your business.

### **Phase 2: Business Management (The Dashboard Flow)**
1. **Log In:** You log into the MeuQR Business OS.
2. **Access CRM:** Click the **📅 Agendamentos** tab on the left sidebar.
3. **Review Bookings:** You see the new booking in the "Próximos Agendamentos" table. It displays the customer's name, phone, chosen date/time, and a yellow `Pendente` badge.
4. **Take Action:**
   * **Message:** Click the green WhatsApp icon to instantly message the customer (e.g., "Hi, confirming your appointment!").
   * **Confirm:** Click the blue Check icon. The status badge changes to a green `Confirmado`.
   * **Cancel:** If the slot is unavailable, click the red X icon to mark it as `Cancelado`.
5. **Fulfillment:** Once the service is rendered in real life, the CRM serves as your historical ledger of customer visits.
